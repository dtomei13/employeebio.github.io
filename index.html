    Meet our legal counselors

    Damani Ingram, Esq.

    Damani Ingram Esq. manages The Ingram Firm. Attorney Ingram received his law degree from the University of Baltimore, where he was a member of the Honor Council. Mr. Ingram is a litigator, advisor, and tireless advocate for his clients. He has represented clients in international transactions throughout Africa and the Middle East. In addition, he has provided legal representation for a multitude of clients within the surrounding Baltimore-Washington metropolitan area and throughout the United States.

    Mr. Ingram also serves as a legal advisor to notable non-profit organizations within the State of Maryland and The District of Columbia. Attorney Ingram is a member of the Maryland Bar Association, the District of Columbia Bar Association, the America Association for Justice, and the Maryland Association for Justice. Attorney Ingram has been recognized as a Top 100 attorney by the National Black Lawyers for 2015, 2016, 2017, and 2018. He has also been recognized as a Maryland Super Lawyer for 2017 and 2018

    • Wayne Williams, Esq.

    Wayne Williams Esq. worked for a small local civil litigation firm and a large statewide litigation firm. In his career, Wayne has handled personal lines claims for large and small insurance companies as well as self-insured companies located throughout the country. For those companies, he represented their insureds in suits involving automotive accidents resulting in personal injury, premises liability matters resulting in personal injury, construction litigation and claims for fraud, defamation and breach of contract. Additionally, Mr. Williams has been involved in litigation throughout the Commonwealth of Virginia.

    Wayne also represents clients in traffic cases in all Hampton Roads jurisdictions and surrounding areas and Accomack and Northampton Counties on the Eastern Shore of Virginia. Wayne is also fully qualified guardian ad litem for incapacitated individuals in civil cases throughout the area. He is admitted to practice in all local state courts and the U.S. District Court for the Eastern District of Virginia. Wayne is a member of the Norfolk and Portsmouth Bar Association, Virginia Beach Bar Association, and the Virginia State Bar. Wayne was the proud recipient of the prestigious Walter E. Hoffman award in 2010.

    • Mu'min Islam, Esq.

    Mu'min Islam Esq. focuses in the areas of personal injury, agency and SROs litigation, and general corporate. Mr. Islam has significant experience representing clients with personal injury matters. Whether it involves premises liability, intentional torts, medical malpractice, or automobile accidents, Mr. Islam represents them all. In addition, Mr. Islam represents clients involved with government agency regulatory actions and special education litigation.

    Mr. Islam's practice also involves the representation of business entities of various sizes. Advising clients on proper entity selection, solicitation and raising of capital, and employment agreements are key aspects of Mr. Islam's corporate practice.
